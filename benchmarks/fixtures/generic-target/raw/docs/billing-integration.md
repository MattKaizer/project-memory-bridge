# Billing Integration

Billing receives authenticated identity as an upstream fact and then computes subscription access, invoice visibility, and entitlement flags.

When a plan changes, the billing flow may publish secondary events for analytics, notifications, and support tooling. Those integrations are structurally relevant in broad reviews even when they are not needed for a focused auth bugfix.

The point of this fixture is to make raw rediscovery meaningfully larger than a compact memory-first pack.
